# Product

## Register

product

## Users
General public and professionals in society who require a highly credible, reliable, and functional AI companion to assist with coding, text generation, and agentic workflows. They need a clutter-free, practical workspace that enables high productivity.

## Product Purpose
To deliver an accessible, stable, and highly capable conversational interface and utility sandbox (Lumina). It empowers users to write code, generate visual assets, and orchestrate agent models with direct, reliable responses, operating similarly to industry-standard expert interfaces like Claude.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Credible, Precise. The tone is executive, academic, and respectful, avoiding casual gaming aesthetics or childish gimmicks.

## Anti-references
- Casual or overly playful consumer interfaces.
- Saturated neon-grid structures that distract from primary layout.
- Playful emoticons, excessive emojis, and informal messaging tones.
- Over-animated decorative elements that disrupt user reading flow.

## Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: Design components, toolbars, and layouts to maximize real-world workspace efficiency and clarity.
2. **Professional Rigor**: Elevate confidence through structured formatting, excellent typography contrast, and robust layout grid systems.
3. **Simplicity of Interaction**: Keep navigation direct, settings logical, and the input flow completely frictionless for all users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High visual contrast (compliance with WCAG AA standards, aiming for ≥4.5:1 on text).
- Simple layout hierarchy to support screen reader navigation and clean visual parsing.
- Built-in options to adapt to different devices with strict mobile and tablet responsiveness.
